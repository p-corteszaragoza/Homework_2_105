About Pets Salon

Our passion for pet care is our core driving spirit that enables us to provide a high-class pet grooming service at your doorstep. We care for your pets just as we would care for our pets. Our mission at Pawsh is to disrupt the pet care delivery system and make it very friendly and transparent.

As we are gestating and rapidly growing, we are proud to state that Pawsh has been chartered to change the world of the grooming service delivery system, and we are working tirelessly to bring you a different experience. Try us once, and you will never like to go anywhere else! We promise you that!
